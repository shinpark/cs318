There and Back Again

We took a two week bacpacking trip through Italy. And during our two week trip we went to four different cities.

  1. Day 1: A brief trip to Florence, Italy
  2. Day 2: A brief trip to Rome, Italy
  3. Day 3: A brief trip to Venice, Italy
  4. Day 4: A brief trip to Pisa, Italy

We really enjoyed our trip and had an amazing time with memories we will never forget. Our favorite city was Venice because of the beautiful water and a ride in a gondola. All in all, we highly reccomend Italy as a travel destination.
